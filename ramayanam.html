RAMAYANAM from study.com 

The title Ramayana is Sanskrit for Ramas Journey.
        
         Ramayana the epic tells the lifes journey of Rama, 
        
         the seventh avatar (incarnation) of the god Vishnu,
         
         from his birth in the kingdom Ayodhya through his years in exile and eventual restoration.
RAMAYANAM from maxwell school 


   Rama, Laksmana, Hanuman, and his monkey army lay siege on Lanka. 
       
      The monkeys make a bridge to Lanka, and after a long battle with spears,
       
      bows and arrows, Rama kills Ravana. Sita, however, is not received by Rama unreservedly;
      
      he questions her chastity after having lived in the house of another man.
